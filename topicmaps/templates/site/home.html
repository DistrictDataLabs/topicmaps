Essential Data Science Topics

From programmers who know statistics, to statisticians who know how to program; everyone that does data science work has their own unique set of skills and tools that they use to get their jobs done. We want to know the essential data science topics that you use in your work.

In this survey, we'll provide you with very open-ended questions asking about what tools, topics, and techniques you use as a data scientist. Please take a few minutes to tell us which of these are most important in your work. In exchange, we'll aggregate the results into a visualization to see what topics are generally most important to data scientists.

Please note, we will NEVER publish or provide to any third party unaggregated responses or identifying data.
